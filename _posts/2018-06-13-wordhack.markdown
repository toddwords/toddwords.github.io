I founded WordHack in the summer of 2014 to create a communal space where people making language-based digital projects could share their work and meet each other. The format consists of 3-4 presenters giving short talks / performances (~15 mins) and an **Open Projector** where anyone from the audience can sign up to present their work for 5 minutes or less.

WordHack happens in NYC on the third thursday of every month at [Wonderville](https://wonderville.nyc) (1186 Broadway in Bushwick).  The show was based out of [Babycastles](https://babycastles.com) for its first 6 years, moved online during the pandemic before landing at its current home at Wonderville. I have been hosting and curating the series since July of 2014.
